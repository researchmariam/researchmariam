Introduction

Dear Reader,

My name is Mariam Azwa Yazid, and I am a PhD candidate at the Faculty of Computer Science and Information Technology, Universiti Putra Malaysia, Serdang. I am currently conducting a research study focused on enhancing the web navigation experience for visually impaired users. The goal of this research is to contribute to the development of more accessible websites that effectively meet the needs of individuals with disabilities, particularly those who are visually impaired.

This website was developed to test the Web Accessibility Interaction Experience (WAIX) Model which emphasizes the elements involved in the interaction process.

Kindly navigate this website and rate your experience by answering the survey.
